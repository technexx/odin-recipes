Eggs Over Easy

This is a very simple recipe for eggs. So simple, it's less a recipe and more of a "heat and serve" sort of thing.

You will need:

  • 2-4 eggs
  • Butter or oil
  • Salt and pepper (optional)
  • A (preferably non-stick) pan

How to make:

  1. Preheat your pan on medium heat
  2. Add butter or oil
  3. Crack eggs onto pan
  4. Allow to cook until edges start to curl and the egg whites are solidified
  5. Flip over
  6. Cook for several more minutes until desired consistency
