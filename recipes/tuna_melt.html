Eggs Over Easy

This is a quick and easy tuna melt recipe.

You will need:

  • A 3-5 oz can of tuna
  • Two slices of bread
  • Cheese of your choosing
  • Butter and oil
  • Two pans

How to make:

  1. Preheat both pans
  2. Butter bread on both sides
  3. In first pan, add bread
  4. In second pan, add oil and tuna
  5. Allow tuna to cook, stirring and mixing as needed
  6. Allow bread to toast on first side
  7. When first side of bread is toasted, flip and add a slice of cheese
  8. When cheese is melted and second side of bread is toasted, remove from pan
  9. When tuna is fully cooked, season and remove from pan and onto bread
  10. Add extras as desired (mayo, mustard, sauerkraut, etc.)
